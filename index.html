ABOUT TEMPLE

🌺 Jai Maa Jawalpa Devi 🌺

Maa Jawalpa Devi ke pavitra dham mein aapka hardik swagat hai. Yah mandir shraddha, bhakti aur seva ka prateek hai. Shri Jawalpa Devi Mandir Samiti ka uddeshya Sanatan Sanskriti ka sanrakshan, dharmik karyakramon ka ayojan, samaj seva tatha sabhi shraddhaluon ko adhyatmik shanti aur Maa ka ashirwad pradan karna hai.

Har varsh hazaron shraddhalu Maa Jawalpa Devi ke darshan aur ashirwad prapt karne ke liye mandir aate hain. Ve dharmik utsavon mein bhag lete hain aur samaj seva evam mandir ke vikas mein apna mahatvapurn yogdan dete hain.

Mandir Ki Sevaayein

Maa Jawalpa Devi ki seva, bhakti aur samaj seva ke liye samarpit.

Nitya Pooja Aur Aarti

Pratidin subah aur shaam Maa Jawalpa Devi ki pooja, aarti aur bhajan ka ayojan kiya jata hai.

Vishesh Pooja

Bhakton ke liye vishesh pooja, havan, sankalp aur dharmik anushthan ki suvidha uplabdh hai.

Bhandara Seva

Dharmik utsavon aur vishesh avsaron par nishulk bhandara evam prasad vitran kiya jata hai.

Dharmik Karyakram

Bhajan-Kirtan, Bhagwat Katha, Ramayan Path aur anya dharmik karyakramon ka niyamit ayojan hota hai.
