Shri Jawalpa Devi Mandir Ka Naveen Nirman

Shri Jawalpa Devi Mandir Samiti ek bhavya, vishal aur adhyatmik mandir ke nirman ke liye nirantar prayasrat hai. Hamara uddeshya sabhi shraddhaluon ke liye ek pavitra evam suvidha-yukt dharmik sthal ka nirmaan karna hai.

Is nirman pariyojana mein mukhya mandir, yagya shala, dhyan kaksh, samudayik bhawan, parking suvidha, evam shraddhaluon ke liye anya aavashyak suvidhaon ka nirmaan kiya ja raha hai.
