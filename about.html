HAMARE MANDIR KE BAARE MEIN

Maa Jawalpa Devi Mandir Samiti

Shri Maa Jawalpa Devi Mandir ek pavitra dharmik sthal hai jo Maa Jawalpa Devi ko samarpit hai. Mandir ka sanchaalan Shri Jawalpa Devi Mandir Samiti dwara kiya jata hai. Samiti ka mukhya uddeshya mandir ki dekhbhal, dharmik paramparaon ka sanrakshan, Maa ki nitya pooja-archana aur samaj seva ke karyon ko badhava dena hai.

Pratyek varsh hazaron shraddhalu Maa Jawalpa Devi ke darshan aur ashirwad prapt karne ke liye mandir aate hain. Yahan bhajan-kirtan, havan, yagya, bhandara tatha vibhinna dharmik evam sanskritik karyakramon ka ayojan kiya jata hai.
